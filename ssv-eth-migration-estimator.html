Important Notes

📊 ETH vs SSV Payments

The transition to ETH-denominated payments aligns validator rewards with operational costs. Clusters now fund operations directly in ETH, making runway calculations more predictable.

Network Fee = Validators × 0.00928 ETH/year
Total Fees = (Operator Fees + Network Fees) / 365 days

🎯 Effective Balance (EB) Accounting

Fees scale with actual validator effective balances, not just validator count. Post-Pectra validators can secure more than 32 ETH, increasing fee accrual accordingly.

Annual Fees = (Operators Fee per Validator) × Validators + (Network Fee per Validator) × Validators

⚡ Liquidation Parameters for ETH Clusters

Network Fee: 1% (~0.00928 ETH) | Liquidation Threshold: ~7 days (~16,000 blocks) | Blocks Per Day: ~7,200

Liquidation Collateral is calculated using the block-based formula:

Liquidation Collateral = (Annual Fees / Blocks Per Year) × LTP
where LTP = Liquidation Threshold Period in blocks

ETH clusters have more capital-efficient liquidation parameters compared to SSV clusters due to all aspects being denominated in ETH.

📋 Pre-Migration Requirements

Before migration, ensure sufficient runway funding. Existing SSV clusters cannot be modified (no validator additions, no runway extensions) until migrated. Migration is one-way and irreversible.
